Structural Engineering

The Structural Engineering Division is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities for testing construction materials and analyzing structural components. Our expert team provides consultancy for the design, assessment, and retrofitting of various civil engineering structures.

We ensure that all tests adhere to international standards such as ASTM, AASHTO, and BNBC to guarantee the safety and durability of infrastructure projects.

Key Services & Tests

  • Compressive Strength of Concrete (Cylinder/Cube)
  • Tensile Strength of Mild Steel (Rebar)
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) / Rebound Hammer
  • Structural Audit & Vulnerability Assessment
  • Mix Design of Concrete
  • Load Testing of Beams and Slabs
  • Design of Multi-storied Buildings (RC & Steel)

Transportation Engineering

Our Transportation Engineering wing supports national infrastructure development through rigorous material testing for roads, highways, and runways. We specialize in the design of flexible and rigid pavements and the analysis of bituminous materials.

We also offer consultancy services for traffic volume studies, intersection design, and road safety audits to improve urban and regional mobility.

Key Services & Tests

  • Bitumen Penetration & Softening Point Test
  • Aggregate Impact Value (AIV) & Crushing Value
  • Los Angeles Abrasion Test
  • Marshall Mix Design for Bituminous Pavements
  • California Bearing Ratio (CBR) Test
  • Traffic Impact Assessment (TIA)
  • Highway Geometric Design

Geotechnical Engineering

The Geotechnical Engineering Division provides comprehensive soil investigation and foundation design services. Understanding the ground beneath is crucial for any construction, and our laboratory offers precise soil characterization.

From field exploration to advanced laboratory testing, we help determine the bearing capacity and settlement characteristics of soil to ensure safe foundation designs.

Key Services & Tests

  • Sub-soil Investigation & Drilling
  • Grain Size Analysis & Hydrometer Test
  • Atterberg Limits (Liquid & Plastic Limit)
  • Direct Shear Test & Triaxial Compression
  • Consolidation & Permeability Test
  • Pile Load Test (PDA & PIT)
  • Slope Stability Analysis

Environmental Engineering

We are committed to sustainable development through our Environmental Engineering services. Our laboratory is equipped to analyze water quality parameters and assess environmental impacts of development projects.

We provide solutions for water supply, wastewater treatment, and solid waste management to ensure public health and environmental protection.

Key Services & Tests

  • Water Quality Analysis (pH, Arsenic, Iron, BOD, COD)
  • Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA)
  • Effluent Treatment Plant (ETP) Design
  • Air & Noise Pollution Monitoring
  • Solid Waste Management Planning
  • Drainage & Sewerage System Design

Water Resource Engineering

The Water Resource Engineering division focuses on the management and regulation of water cycles. We offer expertise in hydrological modeling, flood control, and the design of hydraulic structures.

Our goal is to provide sustainable solutions for river management, irrigation, and urban drainage challenges facing Bangladesh.

Key Services & Tests

  • Hydrological Data Analysis & Modeling
  • River Bank Protection Design
  • Flood Management & Control Systems
  • Irrigation Channel & Canal Design
  • Urban Drainage Master Planning
  • Hydro-morphological Study

Construction Engineering & Management

Efficient project management is key to the success of any construction venture. Our Construction Management division offers expertise in planning, scheduling, and controlling project costs and quality.

We assist clients in optimizing resources, managing risks, and ensuring timely completion of projects through modern management techniques.

Key Services & Tests

  • Project Planning & Scheduling (CPM/PERT)
  • Cost Estimation & Budgeting
  • Construction Supervision & Monitoring
  • Quality Control & Assurance Plans
  • Procurement & Contract Management
  • Construction Safety Management
