About CRTC

Center for Research, Testing, and Consultation

Who We Are

The Center for Research, Testing, and Consultation (CRTC) is the dedicated research and commercial extension of the Department of Civil Engineering, Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST). Established to foster strong collaboration between academia and industry, CRTC is committed to transforming academic knowledge into practical engineering solutions that support sustainable infrastructure development.

CRTC serves as a center of excellence for advanced material testing, structural evaluation, geotechnical investigation, and engineering consultancy. By integrating academic expertise with state-of-the-art laboratory facilities, the center ensures reliable, accurate, and industry-compliant services for both public and private sector projects.

Since its inception, CRTC has made significant contributions to the planning, design, quality control, and safety assessment of infrastructure projects across the Pabna region and beyond. The center regularly provides technical services and consultancy to prominent government organizations, including the BWDB, LGED, PWD, and RHD, alongside leading private construction and engineering firms.

Our laboratories are equipped with modern, high-precision testing instruments capable of conducting a wide range of tests in strict compliance with internationally and nationally recognized standards such as ASTM, AASHTO, BNBC, and BS. Supported by experienced faculty members, skilled engineers, and trained technical staff, CRTC ensures accuracy, integrity, and professionalism in every service it delivers.

Through research, testing, and consultation, CRTC remains dedicated to promoting innovation, safety, and quality assurance in civil engineering practice, while contributing to the sustainable development of Bangladesh’s infrastructure sector.

Organizational Structure

CRTC operates under the direct supervision of the Department of Civil Engineering. The Chairman of the department serves as the Ex-Officio Director of CRTC. A team of professors, associate professors, assistant professors, lecturers works as technical consultants, ensuring the highest level of accuracy and professional integrity in every report generated.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, accurate, and timely testing and consultancy services that contribute to safe and sustainable infrastructure development in Bangladesh.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in civil engineering research and testing, fostering innovation and technical leadership in the region.

Quality Assurance

We adhere to strict quality control protocols. All equipment is calibrated regularly, and testing procedures follow standardized international codes to ensure validity.

Digital Services

In line with the "Smart Bangladesh" vision, CRTC has integrated a modern digital platform to enhance client convenience and transparency:

  • Online Application Tracking: Clients can check the real-time status of their testing samples using a unique Application ID.
  • Report Verification: To ensure authenticity and prevent forgery, all test reports can be verified online using the Report ID generated by our system.
