Welcome to CRTC

Bridging Academia with Industry

The Department of Civil Engineering at Pabna University of Science and Technology (PUST) is dedicated to excellence in education and research. The Center for Research, Testing, and Consultation (CRTC) acts as the bridge between our academic expertise and the nation's infrastructure development.

We provide high-quality consultancy including structural design, soil testing, environmental assessment, and material testing. Our team consists of highly qualified faculty members committed to precision and integrity. We ensure that every test performed in our lab adheres to international standards (ASTM, AASHTO, BNBC).

What We Do

Our Areas of Expertise

Specialized research, testing, and consultation services across six major divisions.

Structural Engineering

Analysis, design, NDT, and seismic assessment of structures.

Transportation Eng.

Pavement design, highway material testing, and traffic studies.

Geotechnical Eng.

Sub-soil investigation, pile load testing, and foundation design.

Environmental Eng.

Water quality analysis, EIA, and waste management plans.

Water Resource Eng.

Hydrological modeling, flood management, and river protection.

Construction Mgmt.

Project supervision, cost estimation, scheduling, and safety.

About CRTC, PUST

We are the leading testing and consultancy body in the Pabna region, officially affiliated with the Dept. of Civil Engineering, Pabna University of Science and Technology.
